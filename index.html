Recycle for a better tomorrow

This Coalescence, we pledge to bring a change in the outlook of those around us and bring awareness about the need of the hour. Our motto here being: Recycle, Reuse and Reduce

  • Recycle- Recycling is the first and most effective of th three R's.
  • Reuse- Rather than throwing out waste items, one can find new uses for them.
  • Reduce- It is the third R and invoves reducing the consumption of itmes.

Coalescence through the years

Over the years, Coalescence has emerged to be undoubtedly one of the largest chemical fests of Eastern India. This year would celebrate the fourth edition of the fest.

Dignitaries like Mr. Rajeev Kumar Singh, Assistant General Manager, R&D Centre of Iron and Steel Authority of India, Prof. Sri Bandopadhyay from School of Material Science and Engineering UNSW, Australia, Prof. Sandip Roy from Department of Chemical Engineering, IIT Bombay and Mr. Tridib Sarkar from IOCL, New Delhi have been a part of Coalescence.

We believe this legacy shall continue and Coalescence will continue growing with leaps and bounds.
