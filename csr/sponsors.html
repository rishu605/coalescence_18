CSR activities: Green and clean, the perfect scene at Panchauli

There goes an old English saying “A picture is worth a thousand words”. Sometimes the best means to communicate a thought or idea, which is bound to leave a big impact on the society, is conveyed via sketches. Keeping these points in mind, Team Coalescence'17 in active collaboration with Dristi, had organized a painting competition among the schools in Panchauli, located in the vicinity of the campus. The competition had been organized, in the perspective of the social cause “Green and Clean- the perfect scene” for Coalescence.

The concept of painting competition was aimed to provide an insight into the minds of people the need of hygiene and cleanliness. The competition had witnessed immense participation from the neighboring schools, and the participants had shown immense creativity in forging their ideas in accordance with the theme.The importance of clean environment, self and public hygiene as well as ideas of combating local pollution were also discussed both in audio-visual form. .We are very thankful to Dristi for Supporting our Social Cause
