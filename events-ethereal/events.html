Coalescence '18 Events

This page lists all the events we will be hosting this year. Check out the eligibility and the event details for the respective events here.

Illuminati

Eligibility

Open to all branches, irrespective of year of study

Event Layout and Rules

It is a paper presentation contest in which a number of topics in different categories are given to the participants and an abstract is expected on any of the topics. The participants will be asked to prepare a short presentation on their papers which is supposed to be given on the day of the competition. A cumulative score will be given based on the answers as well as the overall paper presentation of the participants.

Chemblaze

Eligibility

Open to all branches, irrespective of year of study

Event Layout and Rules

This is a quiz event with an aim to test the technical awareness of the participants. The event is conducted in two rounds. The rounds will have questions from various disciplines like nuclear technology, process optimization and elementary principles of chemical engineering.

Chem-E-Maze

Eligibility

Open to all branches, irrespective of year of study

Event Layout and Rules

Participants will be provided with a combination of riddles and puzzles to get their exact locations, while judgement will be done on the basis of "first come, first serve". Here, participants will be given a virtual tour of the campus with hidden barriers and twisting passages, some of which may seem difficult but in reality these will be places where they throng frequently. This is a fun event giving some respite from the core technical issues.

Doodle-A-Doodle

Eligibility

Open to all branches, irrespective of year of study

Event Layout and Rules

This is an online event. Participants will be given certain situations which have engineering applications. They will be required to correlate these small pictures to get the big picture beind the entire setup. For example, if we show a picture of disfigured bodies, exploded container, heart, India and the chemical structure of a compund containing NCO group, then a guess can be the "Bhopal Gas Tragedy".

Contact: Niraj - 7762973431

Chem-E-Car

Eligibility

Open to all branches, irrespective of year of study

Event Layout and Rules

The participants will have to come prepared with their chem-e-car at the arena. Inspection will be done before the commencement of the event. All participants must remain present during the inspection at least one hour before the commencement of the event. Arena will remain open for practice runs. Points will be awarded on the basis of chemicals used, chemical reaction, design as well as justification for the design parameters used.

Green City Challenge

Eligibility

Open to all branches, irrespective of year of study

Event Layout and Rules

The participants will be given a certain area in our college like IC area, certain department or a hostel. They will have to find out the major energy losses there and suggest changes. The first round consists of the submission of t abstract of their proposal(250 words). The selected candidates will then have to present their proposal if their abstract is selected.

Rasayan Negotium

Eligibility

Open to all branches, irrespective of year of study

Event Layout and Rules

This event involves an Industrial Auction scenario in which participants will bid for several components according to the Industrial Chemical Process assigned to them. Each component will have some base price value and certain specific characteristics, advantages and drawbacks. Participants will have to assess all these factors and bid economically and complete their process setup.
